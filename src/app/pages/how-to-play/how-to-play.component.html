Objective

The objective of the game is to accept missions and defeat the enemies in them.

How to play

On the mission screen, choose the mission you want to accept. Once you accept a mission, you will automatically be directed to the battle screen where you will face the enemy for that mission.

To attack, you must press the attack button and wait for the animations to finish before you can attack again.

When your attack animation is over, it will be the enemy's turn to attack you.

Victory condition

If you reduce the enemy's life to 0, you win the battle.

Defeat condition

If your life is reduced to 0, you lose the battle.

At the end of the battle

After finishing the battle you will be directed to the mission screen.

If you have won the fight you will get experience, you can check how much experience you have in the Profile, when you level up the experience counter is reset

Other features

If you close the game in the middle of a battle, when you log back in, selecting Missions will redirect you to the battle.

When creating your account, you'll need to choose the job and the look you want for that job. Once selected, you can't change it.

From the profile menu, you can log out and delete your account. Please note that deleting your account will delete all your data from the server, and you will not be able to recover it.
